How Our web App Enhances Testing Experience.

What is CBT?

CBT stands for Computer Based Testing. It allows examiners to create and administer tests online. Students can take tests conveniently from their devices.

How do I sign up?

To sign up, visit the registration page. You can create an account as either an examiner or a student. Simply follow the prompts to complete your registration.

Can I reset my password?

Yes, you can reset your password if you forget it. Click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

Is there a calculator?

Yes, our web app includes a calculator feature. This tool is available during tests to assist students. It helps to simplify calculations and improve test performance.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team through the 'Contact Us' option in the ‘about us’ page. Fill out the form with your inquiry. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
