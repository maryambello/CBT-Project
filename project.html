FAQS

Here are some common questions about our Computer Based Test web application

What is CBT?

CBT stands for Computer Based Testing it allows examiners to create and administer tests online.Students
can take test conveniently from thier devices


How do I Sign up?

To sign up, visit the registration page. you can create an account as either an examiner or a student. simply
follow the prompt to complete your registration.


Can I reset my password?

Yes, you can reset your password if you forget it, click on the Forget password link on the Login page
Follow the instructions sent to your registered email

Is there a calculator?

Yes our web app include a calculator features.This tool is available during test to assist student.It helps
to simplify calculations and improve test performance.

How do i contact support?

You can contact our support team through the "Contact Us"option in the "About Us"page.Fill out the form
with your inquiry.We aim to respond within 24 hours.
